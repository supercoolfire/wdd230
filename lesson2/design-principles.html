Alignment

Youtube

All the thumbnails are aligned horizontally and vertically. Side navigation is also aligned.

White Space

Microsoft

https://www.microsoft.com

Microsoft is noticeably using white space effectively. Every page is neat and clean. The fonts are easy to read. The menu items have proper spacing that makes them compatible with the touch screen.

Hick’s Law

Newgrounds

https://www.newgrounds.com/

K.I.S.S. Keep It Short and Simply Complicated. The last time I played games on this website was in 2003. The overall design was catchy and there’s a lot happening in it. There are a lot of ads. but now it's simpler than before. You can find categorized games on one page.
