Target Audience

  • Who: The target audience is of legal age, business owners, or customers, and someone who want to start a business in Gas City. A customer can be another business owner or a consumer. All walks of life. Everyone have equal opportunity to grow and prosper.
  • Age: 18 and above
  • Technology: Familiar with basic feature fo social media using mobile or desktop Income:

Two Personas

  • Business Owner

    Demographics and Education: 50 years old, undisclosed amount of income, Have a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration.He is an owner of Gas Sucks Co. A company that extracts fossil gas. He've been into a lot of city but in this city He thinks he belongs. He refine his own gas and sells raw gas to other refinery. He also manufacture gas container for his product and for other company. That makes his product available to everyone with the help of this chamber. This chamber helps him start his dream busines up and running.

    Technology: He’s been too long in the business, he saw the evolution of computer and can easily adopt in ever changing technology. He is always on the go, 2 in 1 tablet is his preferred device, iPad to be exact.

  • Customer

    Demographics and Education: 30 years old, bachelor's degree in materials science and engineering. He is a resident of Gas City, starting a busines that focused on research and development called Fumes Advanced Refinery Technology(F.A.R.T.). His new innovative machineries are being used by refineries. He repeatedly buy gas by bulk for testing and their byproduct is Their product. He is being helped by the chamber to connect to the right businesses.
